New Character

Nilou is a beautiful dancer from Sumeru.

Nilou is a talented dancer from the region of Sumeru in Genshin Impact. Known for her graceful and mesmerizing movements, Nilou brings peace and joy to those around her. Her outfit, a stunning blend of blue and gold, reflects her connection to the Hydro element, with each dance resembling the harmonious flow of water.

Element

As a Hydro element character, Nilou excels in wide-area attacks through rhythmic dance and water explosions. Her abilities allow players to create a sequence of attacks that deal significant damage while providing essential support to the team.

Character Appearance

Nilou has long, striking red hair and deep blue eyes that enhance her mysterious and captivating aura.

Get to Know Nilou

Explore the world of Sumeru with Nilou, a character who not only masters the art of dance but also wields the power of the water element.
